Traditional None Touch Screen Dashboard vs Touch Screen Dashboards

Get Started
October 4, 2018 Kedoo Lightbody

This is an interview with Ellen Plas at who attends The New School

Ellie Plass a student in the Journalism and Design program gives her opinion on traditional car dashboards and the new touch screen featured dashboards

"It is really unsafe and distracting rather than traditional buttons. Touch screen you have to look at the screen while driving," says Ellie Plass

How often do you use car dashboards?- "In High school when driving to school, and going out with friends."

Is there a potential threat? "Distracted driving is almost as bad as drunk driving. Phones have caused a lot of deaths and the dashboard screen isn't that far away from the phone.""

What is the mental difference between traditional and the whole screen dashboard? "Touch screen is more of a focus rather than the traditional. The traditional has buttons, which you can feel for while driving."

How could it be better- Improvement of the steering wheel. When you are driving you dont even have to take your hands off of the steering wheel; you can just control the multimedia and the car system.

According to NPR, "The touch screen systems in new vehicles are distracting, increasing the risk of accidents. That's according to a new study from the University of Utah. Researchers say all 30 systems they tested cause some level of driver distraction. A prominent safety advocate says automakers can make their systems safer by preventing drivers from using certain features when the car is moving."
